Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bite-Sized Bistro started as a small family restaurant with a passion for great food and excellent service.

Over the years, we've grown into a beloved local establishment, known for our delicious, freshly-prepared dishes and warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our chefs use only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to create memorable culinary experiences for all our guests.
